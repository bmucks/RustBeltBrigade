Rust Belt Brigade

Originally an acoustic punk duo frequently found busking the streets of Buffalo, Rust Belt Brigade has since become a 4 piece Americana outfit carving a sound all their own described to be “as rustic and lilting as you'd expect from a classic folk group while still maintaining the groups punk side”-Buffablog 2017. Since it's formation, Rust Belt has grown to include 2 new members on bass and banjo, and continues grow in music and support.
